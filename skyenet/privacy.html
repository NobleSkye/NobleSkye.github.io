Privacy Policy

At SkyeNet, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it while you play on our Minecraft server.

SkyeNet Minecraft Server Privacy Policy
Effective Date: November, 28, 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you connect to and play on the SkyeNet Minecraft server, we collect the following types of information:

  • Minecraft Username: Your in-game username is required for access to the server and its features.
  • IP Address: We may use your IP address for server management and security purposes, including banning or limiting access to players who violate server rules.
  • Player Activity: We may collect data related to your actions in-game (e.g., movements, chats, items, and achievements) to ensure fair play and improve the server experience.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We may use cookies or similar technologies to track session data on our website or through any associated services (such as a server website or forums).
2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Server Management: To ensure proper operation, maintenance, and improvement of the SkyeNet server.
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and respond to security breaches or violations of server rules and policies.
  • Communication: To notify you about important updates, events, or changes related to the server or community.
3. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. We may retain certain data (such as your Minecraft username or IP address) in our logs for security, moderation, and troubleshooting.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share your personal information with third-party companies for marketing purposes. However, we may use third-party services for certain features, such as:

  • Server Hosting: Your IP address and other basic connection information may be handled by our hosting provider for server performance and management.
  • Discord or Other Community Platforms: If you connect to our community on platforms like Discord, your information is subject to the privacy policies of those platforms. Please review their policies for more details.
5. Children’s Privacy

Our server is not intended for children under the age of 13. If you are under 13 years old, please do not attempt to use or access the server. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to remove that information.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information. If you wish to review, update, or delete your data, please contact us at support@skyenet.co.in.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website or announced in the server. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@skyenet.co.in.

By joining the SkyeNet server, you acknowledge and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
