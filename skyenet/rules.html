Server Rules

These are our official server rules. Please read carefully! Ignorance does not protect you from punishment.

Home
  1. No DDOSing the Server
    Attacks that disrupt or harm the server, including Distributed Denial of Service (DDOS) attacks, are strictly forbidden. Any attempt to bring down the server will result in a permanent ban.
  2. No Chunk Banning
    Chunk banning (intentionally blocking server regions or causing server instability through chunk-related exploits) is prohibited. Such actions are considered abusive and will result in severe consequences.
  3. No Exploiting Newlines in Syncing Chat
    Do not exploit the synchronization between Discord and Minecraft chats, such as using newlines to manipulate chat messages or create misleading impressions. This behavior is considered disruptive and is not allowed.
  4. No Tools for Cracking or Sharing Server Seeds
    Using or sharing tools that crack or reveal server seeds or world generation data is prohibited. If you discover any such information, do not share it. Report it to staff immediately.
  5. Staff Has the Final Say
    Always adhere to the guidance of staff members. If you believe a staff member is acting unfairly or abusing their power, report it to a higher staff member through the ticket system (⁠make-a-ticket) for a review.
  6. All Discord Rules Apply
    The same rules that apply to the SkyeNet Discord server (view them in ⁠⛔rules) also apply on the Minecraft server. Be consistent in your behavior across platforms.
  7. No Hacking or Exploiting
    Using cheats, hacks, or external programs to gain an unfair advantage is strictly forbidden. If you discover an exploit, report it immediately via the ticket system (⁠make-a-ticket). Do not use or take advantage of it.
  8. No Trapping or Killing for No Reason
    Do not trap or kill other players without a valid reason. Light-hearted pranks are allowed, as long as they are harmless and do not cause distress to others.
  9. No Griefing
    Do not destroy or damage another player’s builds or steal their items. Intentionally disrupting someone else's progress is not allowed. If an accidental event occurs (e.g., a creeper explosion), repair the damage and leave a sign explaining what happened.
  10. No Players Under 13
    Due to Discord’s Terms of Service, players must be at least 13 years old to participate in the server. Any account found to be violating this rule will be banned.
  11. Respect Everyone
    Treat all players with kindness and respect. Harassment, bullying, or any form of disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.
